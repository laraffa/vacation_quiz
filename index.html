The vacation destination of your dreams is...

Kauai, Hawaii

You love sun and surf! You're a fan of relaxing, laid-back destinations to kick back and enjoy with friends and family. Kauai is the place for you! Kauai is an island in the mid-Pacific, part of the Hawaiian archipelago. It's nicknamed "the Garden Isle" thanks to the tropical rainforest covering much of its surface. The dramatic cliffs and pinnacles of its Na Pali Coast have served as backdrop for major Hollywood films, while 10-mile-long Waimea Canyon and the Nounou Trails traversing the Sleeping Giant ridge are hiking destinations.

The vacation destination of your dreams is...

Paris, France

Your vacation dream is Paris, France! The city of art and romance! You enjoy history and high culture, museums and monuments. Paris is the city for you. Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture, and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

The vacation destination of your dreams is...

Shanghai, China

Your a city person with a sense of adventure. You like people watching and site seeing. Shanghai is your dream destination!Enormous Shanghai, on China’s central coast, is the country's biggest city and a global financial hub. Its heart is the Bund, a famed waterfront promenade lined with colonial-era buildings. Across the Huangpu River rises Pudong’s futuristic skyline, including 632m Shanghai Tower and the Oriental Pearl TV Tower, with distinctive pink spheres. Sprawling Yuyuan Garden has traditional pavilions, towers and ponds.

The vacation destination of your dreams is...

Aspen, Colorado

You are an ourdoor adventurer who would rather ski fresh powder than lay on the beach all day. Aspen with it's beauty and brawn is the place you want to be. Aspen, in Colorado’s Rocky Mountains, is a ski resort town and a year-round destination for outdoor recreation. It's known for its high-end restaurants, boutiques, and landmarks like the 1889 Wheeler Opera House, built during the area’s silver mining boom. Its Wheeler-Stallard House is a local history museum set in a 19th-century, Queen Anne–style home, while the Aspen Art Museum displays works from contemporary artists.
